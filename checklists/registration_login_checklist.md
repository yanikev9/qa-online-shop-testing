# Registration and Login Checklist
## Registration
- Registration with valid email and password
- Registration with already registered email
- Registration with invalid email format
- Registration with empty email field
- Registration with empty password field
- Registration with password less than 8 characters
- Registration with password more than allowed length
- Registration with special characters in password
- Registration with spaces in email
- Registration with SQL injection in email field
- Registration with script injection (XSS)

## Login
- Login with valid credentials
- Login with incorrect password
- Login with unregistered email
- Login with empty email field
- Login with empty password field
- Login with spaces in credentials
- Login after multiple incorrect attempts
- Login after account lock
